About
Back in 2016, I took a leap into the world of software development with a mission to help students in their programming journey using Visual Studio C# and Netbeans. Little did I know that this decision would lead me on an exciting adventure, diving headfirst into a world of endless possibilities.

As time went by, I had the incredible opportunity to build software for all kinds of businesses. From startups and gyms to catering stores, jewelry services, fitness & wellness centers, e-commerce platforms, and even enterprise-grade hospital, and restaurant, management systems. Let me tell you, it's been quite a thrilling ride!

These days, my main focus is on building powerful products and leading projects for businesses that crave secure, fast, reliable, and user-friendly software services. I thrive on delivering solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations, making a real impact in the process. But hey, life isn't just about coding! When I'm not diving deep into lines of code, you can find me devoting 2-4 hours of my free time to studying new technologies or enjoying random videos from fireship.io. And when I feel the need to take a break and clear my mind, I indulge in a few rounds of Dota 2 , a game that never fails to recharge my batteries.

Of course, balance is key. So, when I'm not glued to my computer screen, I make sure to spend quality time with my loved ones. Whether it's creating precious memories with my family, embarking on exciting adventures with my girlfriend, or simply hanging out and having a good time with friends, those moments of connection and joy truly fuel my passion.

That's just a glimpse into my journey so far, and let me tell you, I can't wait to see what exciting projects and experiences lie ahead.

Oh, and before I forget to mention, in addition to my professional work, I've also had the pleasure of building over 40 pro bono projects for bachelor's, master's and even phD programs. Being able to contribute to the education of students by providing practical projects has been an incredibly rewarding experience.

These pro bono projects have spanned various domains, from educational platforms to community-focused applications and social impact initiatives. It's my way of giving back to the programming community and inspiring aspiring engineers on their academic path.

So, as I continue on my journey, I eagerly look forward to embracing new challenges, crafting remarkable software solutions, and making a positive difference in the lives of clients, students, and the broader community. Cheers to the exciting times ahead!
